Our Mission

At Manavata, our mission is to be a lifeline to the forgotten and the vulnerable. We are committed to reaching abused children, orphans, widows, and others in dire need â€” providing compassionate care, practical support, and a path to healing.

Through love, service, and unwavering commitment, we aim to restore dignity, ignite hope, and empower lives. Every act of kindness brings us one step closer to a more just and humane world.

About Manavata

Manavata, meaning "A Foundation for Succour," was established with a clear vision: to create an efficient, effective, and sustainable charitable organization that makes a real difference in people's lives.

Our foundation is built on the principles of integrity, professionalism, teamwork, empathy, quality service, and inclusivity. We believe that by working together with compassion and dedication, we can transform lives and communities.

Our Vision

To have an efficient, effective, and sustainable charitable course.

Our Values

Integrity, Professionalism, Teamwork, Empathy, Quality Service, Diversity and Inclusivity.
